      12 large eggs
      1/2 cup mayonnaise

      1/4 teaspoon salt
      1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    Optional garnish: paprika or chopped parsley Steps:
      Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with cold water.
      Bring to a boil.
      Once boiling, remove from heat, cover, and let sit for 12 minutes.
    • Drain hot water and transfer eggs to an ice bath. Let cool completely, then peel. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and carefully remove yolks.
